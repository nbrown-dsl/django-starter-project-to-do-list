Skills app

This is a service for all your IT skills needs. It is the place where you can...

  • Find out what IT skills you need for your role
  • Link to resources to learn these skills
  • Find which staff to appraoch who have these skills
  • Keep track of what skills you have acquired
  • Propose skills to learn

It is the place where we can collectlively collate the IT skills we feel we need to deliver high quality personalised education for our students.
